Important: File Format

OPay Wrapped only accepts Excel (.xlsx) or CSV files. PDF files are not supported.

1

Open OPay App

Open your OPay app and go to the home/dashboard screen.

2

Go to Transaction History

Tap on "Transaction History" to see all your past transactions.

3

Tap "Download"

At the top right corner, tap the "Download" button.

4

Select "Custom" Time Frame

Tap the "Custom" tab so you can set it to a full year.

5

Set Start & End Date

Tap "Start Date" → set to Jan 1, 2024. Then "End Date" → set to Dec 31, 2024.

6

Choose File Type

⚡ CRITICAL

Tap "File Type" and select Excel or CSV.

✅ Excel (.xlsx) ✅ CSV ❌ PDF
7

Tap "Continue" & "Send to Email"

Tap "Continue", then "Send to Email" to receive the file.

8

Download from Email & Upload Here

Open your email, download the Excel/CSV file, and upload it here to get your yearly analysis! 🚀
